Finger puppets

Bought a pair once from a Peruvian pushcart, one a nun and the other a magician. They were in love for awhile, until lightning struck a tower and the nun fell from height. Her scarf got snagged in a nail when she fell, so the fall broke her neck. The magician wept and so it rained. I thought that this was the backstory of the tarot card 'The Tower'. We broke up two years later.
